Stage 1 — Neglect

The power phase. 8/8 criteria ideally met. New highs, strong RS, rising MAs. Only stage to BUY.

Stage 3 — Distribution

Top forming. Volume spikes on down days. MA spread narrowing. Take profits. No new longs.

Stage 4 — Declining

Downtrend confirmed. Price below key MAs. Be in cash or short. Do not buy into a Stage 4 decline.

📖 VCP — Minervini's Core Pattern
What is VCP?

Successive price contractions that get progressively tighter, signaling decreasing supply as the stock prepares for a breakout.

Volume Rule

Volume must DECLINE with each contraction. Drying volume = supply exhausting. Spike on breakout = demand surge confirmation.

Notation: 3T 27/8/3

3T = 3 tightenings. 27/8/3 = depth % of each contraction. Tighter final contraction = higher quality VCP.

Entry (Pivot Point)

Buy at the breakout above the tightest contraction high. Volume must be 40-50% above average. This is the optimal low-risk entry.

📖 Trade Planner Guide
R:R ≥ 3 — Ideal

Potential reward ≥ 3× risk. The math is strongly in your favor. This is where Minervini focuses exclusively.

R:R 2–3 — Acceptable

Acceptable for A-tier setups. Be selective. Confirm strong RS, VCP, and confirmed Stage 2.

R:R < 2 — Avoid

The math doesn't work. Even 50% win rate = breakeven at best. Wait for better entry or wider target.
