Saudi Tadawul (TASI) · Earnings Season

Q2 2026 High-Conviction Earnings Preview — 20 Companies

Saudi Arabia's June 2026 PMI rose to 53.3 — the highest business-sentiment reading since January — providing a constructive backdrop for Q2 earnings. The sector-wide provision-release cycle in banking, elevated commodity prices in mining, Aramco's guided downstream strength, and Vision 2030 digital spending converge as the primary earnings drivers this quarter.

Energy

Aramco's downstream EBIT more than tripled in Q1; management guided another strong Q2. Refining margins remain elevated due to market tightness. (132)

Digital / Telecom (4 names)

Saudi IT spending projected to grow ~14% annually through 2028. Elm's Q1 revenue beat at +31.7% YoY sets a high bar for Q2. (133)

Insurance

Mining

Construction / Cement

Petrochemicals

Gradual recovery underway but structural overcapacity limits upside. Logistics rerouting via Yanbu adds cost. (137)
